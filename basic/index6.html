Donation Guidelines
  • ✅ Minimum age: 18 years
  • ✅ Weight must be at least 50 kg
  • ✅ Last donation must be 3 months ago (for whole blood)
  • ✅ No fever or infectious disease at donation time
  • ✅ Avoid heavy meals & alcohol before donation
  • ✅ Bring a valid ID to the donation center
  • ✅ Post-donation rest & fluids recommended
Tip: Click Be a Donor to register as a long-term donor.
