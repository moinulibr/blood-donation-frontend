Kivabe Rokto Dan Korben?

Amader shojho 3-step proccess follow korun ebong jibon bachate sahajjo korun.

1. Register Korun

Amader platform-e ekta account khulun ebong apnar roktodani hishebe register korun.

2. Appointment Theek Korun

Apnar kacher blood donation center e appointment theek korun.

3. Rokto Dan Korun

Appointment moto center e giye rokto dan korun. Prochesta-ti shompurno.

4. Shongjog Rakhun

Apnar rokto diye kar jibon becheche, ta jante parben.
